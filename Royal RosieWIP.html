Update 6 - 2026-03-30

Left eye was feeling too big and too far right, so I erased and redid maybe 10 times. Then it only looked good once I redid the left eye and used other software to edit and test eye positionings. I think it came out more accurate to the real Rosie, but at the cost of eraasing so much of the right eye that the graphite is stuck there and looks more like stroke shading than stippling shading. But, I can sleep at night cuz life in't gonna be all cotton candy and butterflies. Some times it's gonna be erasing an eye 10 times so much that it ruins the main concept of your art.

TLDR: use pens to stipple, sketch in pencil, and spend more time sketching to spend less time correcting.
